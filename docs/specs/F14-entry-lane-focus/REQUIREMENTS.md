# F14 Entry Lane Focus Requirements

## Objective

Reduce the remaining confusion and narrative load in the public entry flow so FIC is obviously the product and the company layer stays supportive rather than competitive.

## Functional Requirements

### Home Page

- the home page should have one clear primary product message
- the home page should have one clear primary CTA into `/portal/signup`
- the home page should keep account access visible, but secondary
- North Star should appear as backing/support, not as a second thing to evaluate
- the home page should minimize supporting paragraphs
- the consultancy route should not compete with the main CTA from the hero
- product signals should be short and scannable

### Signup And Billing

- signup and billing should feel like one coherent onboarding lane
- copy on both pages should be short, direct, and operational
- signup should clearly describe that the step creates the shop account
- billing should clearly describe that it is the final step before the workspace opens

### Non-Goals

- changing the merchant workspace
- removing North Star from company-facing surfaces entirely
- implementing real auth or billing
