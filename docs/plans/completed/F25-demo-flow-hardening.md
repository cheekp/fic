# F25 Demo Flow Hardening

## Goal
- Make the founder-demo lane feel natural from billing to first live programme, with less guesswork about what to do next.

## Scope
- Keep billing to one clear founding tier for now.
- Hand off from billing into first-programme setup explicitly.
- Add a compact guided setup lane through:
  - template choice
  - programme configuration
  - publish and test
- Make the programme operate view more obviously demo-ready with direct join actions.
- Keep the guided lane easy to dismiss once the merchant no longer needs it.

## Out Of Scope
- New delivery channels beyond Apple Wallet.
- Production billing integration.
- Team or staff workflows.
- Broad workspace IA changes outside the first-programme demo lane.

## Proof
- `scripts/validate-f25-demo-flow-hardening.sh`
